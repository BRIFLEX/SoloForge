Digital Solutions in Enterprise Lending

SoloForge Finance Practice · Town Hall Recording · 30 min

In this SoloForge LLC town hall, our finance practice explores how regional lenders and credit unions modernize rural and commercial lending — from paper-heavy origination to fully digital pipelines with automated underwriting and regulatory audit trails.

Topics covered

  • Replacing legacy loan origination systems without regulatory risk — SoloForge's phased migration approach
  • Integrating credit bureaus, appraisal APIs, DocuSign, and core banking in one origination flow
  • Mobile-first applications for field agents serving agricultural and rural borrowers
  • Real-time portfolio dashboards for executive committees and board reporting

Client outcome: 21 days → 6 days

A regional lender partnered with SoloForge to rebuild their commercial lending stack. Average origination dropped from 21 days to 6 days while maintaining full audit trails required by their regulator — zero compliance findings in the first-year examination.
