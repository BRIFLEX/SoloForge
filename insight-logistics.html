Digital Technologies in Logistics

By David Park, COO · SoloForge Logistics Practice · 8 min read

Supply chains generate more telemetry than most industries, yet operators still reconcile in spreadsheets. SoloForge built RouteForge to process 2M daily GPS events — here's the architecture pattern we reuse for every logistics engagement.

The visibility gap

Track-and-trace projects fail when they integrate one carrier or one warehouse. SoloForge normalizes TMS, WMS, and carrier EDI into a single Kafka event stream — dispatchers and executives see the same real-time map.

Mobile-first for drivers

RouteForge's Flutter app works offline for 4+ hours — local SQLite cache, background sync, battery optimization. Drivers won't use apps that lag in rural coverage zones; we test on actual routes before launch.

ML routing with clean data

SoloForge ML route optimization saves 8–15% on fuel when fed six months of baseline telemetry. We won't turn on optimization until data quality passes automated validation — garbage routes cost more than no routes.

Two-week integration audit

SoloForge offers a fixed-scope assessment that maps your systems, identifies the highest-ROI digitization path, and delivers a build estimate. Most clients start here before committing to a full platform build.
