Overview of Healthcare AI Solutions

By Priya Sharma, VP Engineering · SoloForge Health Practice · 18 min read

Healthcare organizations face a paradox: AI promises faster diagnoses and reduced admin burden, yet most pilots never reach production. At SoloForge LLC, we've shipped HIPAA-compliant AI for hospital networks serving 180K+ patients — here's what separates hype from outcomes.

Where SoloForge clients see ROI today

Clinical documentation assistance saves physicians 45–90 minutes per shift. Prior authorization automation cuts approval cycles from 5 days to 8 hours. Patient triage chatbots deflect 30% of routine scheduling calls. These three use cases share structured input data, clear human oversight, and measurable time savings — the criteria SoloForge uses in every health AI assessment.

Governance before models

Before selecting an LLM, SoloForge teams define data lineage, access controls, audit logging, and bias testing protocols. On Health Connect, every AI inference is logged with input hash, model version, and clinician review status — pass an OCR audit or don't go live.

Epic and FHIR integration

US providers require EHR integration via FHIR R4 APIs. SoloForge builds thin integration layers so AI features swap without rewriting clinical workflows — this pattern reduced rollback risk 60% on our largest hospital deployment.

Start with one workflow

SoloForge recommends post-discharge follow-up or appointment scheduling as first use cases — high volume, low clinical risk, 90-day measurement window. Our fixed-scope discovery engagement identifies that use case in two weeks.
